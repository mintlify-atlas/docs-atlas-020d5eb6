## Taking screenshots

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Basic usage">
    ### Capturing screenshots
    
    Press the screenshot key (default: `F2`) to capture your game screen. Essential enhances this with:
    
    - **Instant preview** - See a thumbnail preview of your screenshot immediately
    - **Quick actions** - Hover over the preview for instant actions
    - **Custom sound** - Distinct screenshot sound effect
    - **Async capture** - Screenshots taken without lag on 1.8.9 & 1.12.2
    
    <Info>
      Screenshots are saved to your `.minecraft/screenshots` folder and automatically appear in the Screenshot Manager.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Quick actions">
    ### Screenshot preview actions
    
    When you take a screenshot, hover over the preview toast to access:
    
    - **Upload** - Share your screenshot via link
    - **Copy** - Copy the image to clipboard
    - **Edit** - Open in the screenshot editor
    - **Favorite** - Mark as favorite for easy access
    
    <Note>
      You can configure which quick action runs automatically after taking a screenshot in Essential settings. The default is "Copy Picture".
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Advanced">
    ### Performance optimization
    
    Essential optimizes screenshot capture for better performance:
    
    **On Minecraft 1.8.9 and 1.12.2:**
    - Screenshots are taken asynchronously
    - No lag or frame drops during capture
    - Multiple screenshots can be taken rapidly
    
    **On all versions:**
    - Efficient image processing
    - Background upload capability
    - Optimized storage format
    
    <Warning>
      Screenshots taken while notifications are active may be blank on Minecraft 1.21.5. This has been fixed in recent versions.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Screenshot editor

Edit your screenshots before sharing them with built-in editing tools.

### Editing tools

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Drawing">
    ### Drawing tools
    
    Add annotations and highlights to your screenshots:
    
    - **Freehand drawing** - Draw freely with customizable brush
    - **Color picker** - Choose from full color palette
    - **Brush sizes** - Multiple thickness options
    - **Undo/redo** - Reverse unwanted changes
    - **Clear all** - Start over with original image
    
    Perfect for:
    - Highlighting important areas
    - Adding arrows and annotations
    - Creating guides or tutorials
    - Censoring sensitive information
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Cropping">
    ### Crop tool
    
    Trim your screenshots to focus on what matters:
    
    - **Aspect ratio presets** - Common ratios (16:9, 4:3, 1:1)
    - **Free crop** - Custom dimensions
    - **Precise selection** - Pixel-perfect cropping
    - **Preview** - See result before applying
    
    Use cases:
    - Remove HUD elements
    - Focus on specific details
    - Prepare for social media
    - Create thumbnails
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Saving">
    ### Save options
    
    After editing:
    
    - **Save** - Overwrites original screenshot
    - **Save as new** - Creates a copy
    - **Cancel** - Discard changes
    - **Upload** - Save and share immediately
    
    <Warning>
      Saving will overwrite the original file. Use "Save as new" to preserve the original screenshot.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="Screenshots not appearing">
  **Solutions:**
  - Check `.minecraft/screenshots` folder exists
  - Verify Essential is enabled in settings
  - Check for resource pack conflicts
  - Restart Minecraft
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Blank screenshots on 1.21.5">
  **Cause:** Active notifications during capture
  
  **Solution:** Update to the latest Essential version where this is fixed
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Copy image not working">
  **Platform-specific fixes:**
  - **macOS**: Ensure clipboard permissions are granted
  - **Linux**: May not work reliably with all applications
  - **Windows**: Usually works without issues
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Upload button on toast requires Terms of Service">
  **Solution:** Accept Essential Terms of Service in settings before uploading
</Accordion>
