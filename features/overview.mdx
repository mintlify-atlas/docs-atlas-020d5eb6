Essential is a comprehensive Minecraft mod that enhances your gameplay experience with powerful features for screenshots, cosmetics, multiplayer hosting, and UI improvements.

## Feature highlights

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Screenshots & media" icon="camera">
    - **Screenshot browser** - Browse all screenshots sorted by world or server
    - **Screenshot editor** - Draw on and crop screenshots before saving
    - **Quick actions** - Upload, copy, edit, and favorite from preview toast
    - **Media sharing** - Upload screenshots to share links with friends
    - **Picture library** - View and delete uploaded screenshots from media.essential.gg
    - **Async capture** - Screenshots taken without lag (1.8.9 & 1.12.2)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Wardrobe & customization" icon="palette">
    - **100+ cosmetics** - Extensive collection of character cosmetics
    - **Color customization** - Adjust colors and positions of cosmetics
    - **Emote wheel** - Access and switch between emote wheels in-game
    - **Outfit system** - Create, save, and manage up to 128 outfits
    - **Skin library** - Manage skins via link, file, or username
    - **Cape support** - Official Mojang capes and Essential capes
    - **Gifting** - Send cosmetics and emotes to friends
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Multiplayer & social" icon="users">
    - **World hosting** - Invite friends to singleplayer worlds instantly
    - **Discord integration** - Show activity status and invite via Discord
    - **Social menu** - Message friends, share screenshots, and organize groups
    - **World settings** - Manage gamerules, PvP, permissions, and more
    - **Voice chat support** - Compatible with Plasmo Voice and Simple Voice Chat
    - **Resource pack sharing** - Share your resource packs when hosting
    - **Regional proxies** - Improved connection quality worldwide
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="User interface" icon="desktop">
    - **Redesigned menus** - Modern UI across all screens
    - **Account switcher** - Quickly switch between Minecraft accounts
    - **Friend indicators** - See which servers and worlds friends are on
    - **Enhanced settings** - Organized and improved settings layout
    - **Notification system** - Toast notifications for events and updates
    - **Vanilla compatibility** - Option to use Minecraft button textures
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Platform support

<Info>
  Essential supports multiple Minecraft versions and mod loaders:
  - **Minecraft versions**: 1.8.9, 1.12.2, 1.16.5, 1.17+, up to 1.21.8
  - **Mod loaders**: Forge and Fabric
</Info>

## Essential menu

Access all Essential features through the Essential button on the main menu:

- **Wardrobe** - Customize your character's appearance
- **Screenshot Manager** - Browse and manage your screenshots
- **Social Menu** - Connect with friends and communities
- **Settings** - Configure Essential features and preferences
- **Changelogs** - View update notes and new features

<Note>
  Many features can be accessed via keybinds for quick access. Check the Controls settings to view and customize keybinds.
</Note>

## Privacy & safety

Essential includes comprehensive privacy and safety features:

- Privacy settings for each server individually
- Community rules and reporting system
- User blocking and muting capabilities
- Terms of service and privacy policy protection
- Suspension system for rule violations
