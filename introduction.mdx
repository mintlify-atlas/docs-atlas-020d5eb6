## What is Essential?

Essential is a quality of life mod that enhances your Minecraft Java Edition experience with powerful features. Host worlds for free, chat with friends, create custom outfits, react with emotes, and enjoy many quality of life improvements!

The source code of the Essential Mod is accessible to everyone, demonstrating our commitment to transparency with our users and the broader community.

<Note>
  Essential is currently on version **1.3.10.6** and supports a wide range of Minecraft versions from 1.8.9 to 1.21.11 across Forge, Fabric, and NeoForge mod loaders.
</Note>

## Supported platforms

Essential supports multiple Minecraft versions and mod loaders:

- **Minecraft versions**: 1.8.9, 1.12.2, 1.16.2, 1.17.1, 1.18.1-1.18.2, 1.19-1.19.4, 1.20-1.20.6, 1.21.1-1.21.11
- **Mod loaders**: Forge, Fabric, and NeoForge (1.20.4+)
- **Environment**: Client-side only
